CARRRDS

Carrrds is basic card game that is based on guessing where the Ace of spades is on a deck of 4 cards

The Opening interface

The Middle Game

This is the part where the cards are shuffled for the next round

Game Over

After all rounds are completed, The Game over interface comes next

This project was inspired by our drive to solidfiy our javascript skills and also build a simple card game that everybody can understand and play. Most card games are not beginner-friendly, but Carrrds is user-friendly and easy to play. We decided to use HTML5, CSS3 and Pure Javascript for our projects.
